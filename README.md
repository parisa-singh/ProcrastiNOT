# ProcrastiNOT

**ProcrastiNOT** is an AI-powered productivity companion designed to help college students cut through the chaos of student life and get work done — fast, efficiently, and without burnout. By combining mood tracking, smart task management, and AI-driven focus suggestions, it turns procrastination into meaningful progress.

---

## What It Does

ProcrastiNOT helps students manage their time and energy by understanding their mood, workload, and habits. Features include:

- **Mood Check-In Dashboard:** Log emotions and energy levels; interface adapts dynamically and tracks weekly trends.  
- **Smart Task Manager:** Add tasks with duration, importance, and deadlines; AI prioritizes them efficiently.  
- **Study Log:** Track study sessions and get personalized AI feedback.  
- **AI Focus Suggestions:** Optimized focus periods, breaks, and subtle nudges to boost productivity without guilt.

---

## Inspiration

College life is a constant juggle of classes, clubs, and personal commitments. Most productivity apps feel robotic or guilt-driven. We wanted something different — a friendly mentor that helps students understand *why* they procrastinate and gently guides them to get back on track. That’s how **ProcrastiNOT** was born.
